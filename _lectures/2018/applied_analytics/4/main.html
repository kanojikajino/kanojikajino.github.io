べき乗法¶

  • $A\in\mathbb{R}^{N\times N}$ $\lambda_1,\dots,\lambda_N$, $v_1,\dots,v_N$
  • $|\lambda_1| > |\lambda_2| > \cdots > |\lambda_N|$

$x\in\mathbb{R}^N$ :

$\begin{eqnarray} x = \sum_{n=1}^{N}c_n v_n \end{eqnarray}$

$A$ :

$\begin{eqnarray} A^k x &= \sum_{n=1}^{N}c_n A^k v_n = \sum_{n=1}^N c_n \lambda_n^k v_n\\ &= \lambda_1^k \sum_{n=1}^N c_n \left(\frac{\lambda_n}{\lambda_1}\right)^k v_n\\ &\approx \lambda_1^k c_1 v_1 \end{eqnarray}$

  • $A$ $v_1$
  • $\lambda_1 = \dfrac{v_1^\top A v_1}{v_1^\top v_1}$

主成分分析, PCA¶

$x_1,\dots,x_N\in \mathbb{R}^D$ ""

  • 1002
  • $K~(<D)$

PCA $W, U$ :

  • $x\in\mathbb{R}^D$
  • $z = Wx$ ($W\in\mathbb{R}^{K\times D}$)
  • $\hat{x} = U^\top z = U^\top W x$ ($U\in\mathbb{R}^{K\times D}$)
  • $\hat{x}\approx x$

定式化¶

  • $X = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 & x_2 & \dots & x_N \end{bmatrix}^\top$
  • $0$: $\sum_{n=1}^N x_n = 0$
  • $W, U$

$$ \min_{W, U\in\mathbb{R}^{K\times D}} \sum_{n=1}^{N}\|x_n - U^{\top}Wx_n\|_{2}^2 \tag{1} $$

補題1¶

(1)

$$ \begin{align} \min_{V\in\mathbb{R}^{K\times D},\ VV^\top=I} \sum_{n=1}^{N}\|x_n - V^{\top}Vx_n\|_{2}^2\tag{2} \end{align} $$

証明¶

  • $R = \{U^\top W x \mid x \in \mathbb{R}^D\}$ $R$$\mathbb{R}^D$$K$
  • $R$ $V=\begin{bmatrix}v_1 & \dots & v_K\end{bmatrix}^\top\in\mathbb{R}^{D\times K}$
  • $V^\top Vx = \arg\min_{\tilde{x}\in R} \|x - \tilde{x}\|$
    • $R$ $y\in\mathbb{R}^K$ $V^\top y$
  • (1) $U^\star$, $W^\star$ $R^\star$, $V^\star$ $$ \begin{align} \|x_n - {U^\star}^\top W^\star x_n\|^2 \geq \min_{\tilde{x}_n\in R^\star}\|x_n - \tilde{x}_n\|^2 = \|x_n - {V^\star}^\top V^\star x_n\|^2 \end{align} $$
  • $U^\star, W^\star$ $V^\star$

補題2¶

(2) $$ \max_{V\in\mathbb{R}^{K\times D}, VV^\top=I}\mathrm{tr}\left(V \left(\sum_{n=1}^N x_n x_n^\top\right)V^\top\right) \tag{3} $$

証明¶

$$ \sum_{n=1}^N \|x_n - V^\top V x_n\|^2 = \sum_{n=1}^N \|x_n\|^2 - 2\sum_{n=1}^N x_n^\top V^\top V X_n + \sum_{n=1}^N x_n^\top V^\top V V^\top V x_n $$

$$ =\sum_{n=1}^N \|x_n\|^2 - \sum_{n=1}^N x_n^\top V^\top V x_n $$

$$ \begin{align} \sum_{n=1}^N x_n^\top V^\top V x_n = \sum_{n=1}^N \mathrm{tr}(x_n^\top V^\top V x_n) = \sum_{n=1}^N \mathrm{tr}(x_n x_n^\top V^\top V) \\ = \mathrm{tr}\left(\left(\sum_{n=1}^N x_n x_n^\top\right) V^\top V\right) = \mathrm{tr}\left(V\left(\sum_{n=1}^N x_n x_n^\top\right) V^\top\right) \end{align} $$

補題3¶

$A=\sum_{n=1}^N x_n x_n^\top$ $\lambda_1,\dots, \lambda_D$, $u_1,\dots,u_D$ $\lambda_1\geq\lambda_2\geq\cdots\geq\lambda_D$ $V\in\mathbb{R}^{K\times D}, VV^\top=I$

$$ \mathrm{tr}\left(V A V^\top\right) \leq \max_{w\in[0,1]^D, \sum_{d=1}^D w_d\leq K}\sum_{d=1}^D \lambda_d w_d = \sum_{d=1}^K \lambda_d $$

証明¶

$A=U^\top \Lambda U$ $U\in\mathbb{R}^{D\times D}$ $V\in\mathbb{R}^{K\times D}, VV^\top = I$

$W=VU^\top\in\mathbb{R}^{K\times D}$

  • $VAV^\top = VU^\top\Lambda U V^\top = W\Lambda W^\top$.
  • $W$ $D$: $WW^\top = UV^\top VU^\top = I$

$$ \begin{align} \mathrm{tr}(W\Lambda W^\top) = \sum_{d=1}^D \lambda_d \sum_{k=1}^Kw_{k,d}^2 \end{align} $$

$w_d:=\sum_{k=1}^K w_{k,d}^2$

$$ \begin{align} \mathrm{tr}(W\Lambda W^\top) &= \sum_{d=1}^D \lambda_d \sum_{k=1}^Kw_{k,d}^2\\ &= \sum_{d=1}^D \lambda_d w_d \end{align} $$

$0\leq\sum_{k=1}^K w_{k,d}^2 \leq 1~(d=1,\dots,D)$, $\sum_{d=1}^D \sum_{k=1}^K w_{k,d}^2=K$

$$ \begin{align} \mathrm{tr}(W\Lambda W^\top) &= \sum_{d=1}^D \lambda_d w_d\\ &\leq \max_{w_d\in[0,1], \sum_{d=1}^D w_d\leq K}\sum_{d=1}^D \lambda_d w_d \end{align} $$ .

定理¶

$A=\sum_{n=1}^N x_n x_n^\top$ $\lambda_1,\dots, \lambda_D$, $u_1,\dots,u_D$ $\lambda_1\geq\lambda_2\geq\cdots\geq\lambda_D$

$V = \begin{bmatrix}u_1 \dots u_K\end{bmatrix}^\top$ (2), (3)

証明¶

$$ \mathrm{tr}(VAV^\top) = \mathrm{tr}(VU^\top \Lambda UV^\top) = \mathrm{tr}(\mathbb{1}_K \Lambda \mathbb{1}_K) = \sum_{d=1}^K \lambda_d $$ $$ [\mathbb{1}_K]_{i,j} = \begin{cases} \delta_{i,j} &\text{ if } i=j\leq K\\ 0 & \text{otherwise}. \end{cases} $$

from scipy.linalg import eigh
def pca(X, K):
    A = X.T @ X
    eig_val, eig_vec = eigh(A)
    V = eig_vec[:, -K:]
    z = X @ V
    return z, V
